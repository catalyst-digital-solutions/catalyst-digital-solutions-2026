### Frequently Asked Questions

## Do you require long-term contracts?

No. We operate month-to-month. We keep clients because the results speak — not because the contract traps them.

## What's the realistic timeline to see results?

Most clients see measurable improvement in phone call volume and search ranking within 60–90 days. Content distribution starts immediately. SEO typically shows meaningful movement by month 3.

## Do I need to be tech-savvy to work with you?

No. Your job is to run your construction business. Our job is to handle the digital side completely. We'll need about 3 hours of your time in the first two weeks and maybe 30 minutes per month after that.

## How is this different from just hiring an in-house marketing person?

A competent in-house digital marketer costs $65,000–$85,000/year — and they don't come with AI video pipelines, programmatic SEO infrastructure, or an RFP monitoring system. You'd also need additional tools and platforms on top of their salary. Our Growth Engine tier is a fraction of that cost with more capability.

## Can I start at Tier 1 and upgrade?

Absolutely. Many clients start with Launchpad to establish their foundation, then move to Growth Engine once they see the website performing. The upgrade is seamless — no rebuild required.

## What trades do you work with?

General contractors, commercial builders, specialty trades (electrical, plumbing, HVAC, roofing), civil contractors, and demolition companies. If you bid on projects and need your phone ringing, we can help.

## What happens if I cancel?

You keep everything we built — your website, your content, your analytics setup. We don't hold anything hostage. If you cancel the retainer, the deliverables stay yours.

## Is there a setup fee?

No setup fee. The first month covers onboarding, website deployment, and system configuration. Everything is included in the monthly retainer.
